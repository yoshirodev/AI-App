We're the Eclipse Dev Team - three cousins who love creating software for fun! We're currently working on a low tier anime chatbot called Epsilon. It's not quite ready yet, but we're excited to bring it to life! Our goal is to create a chatbot that's easy to talk to and feels like a real person. We believe that AI can make conversations more fun and interesting, and we want to share that with the world!
